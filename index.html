Compassionate Care Services

In-Home Postpartum Support

Those early weeks after birth can feel both magical and overwhelming. I create a nurturing sanctuary in your home with hands-on recovery support, emotional companionship, and practical help that keeps your household running smoothly.

Evidence-Based Newborn Care

Wondering what your baby is trying to tell you? Babies speak their own language of cues and behaviors. Drawing from specialized training in newborn development, I help you understand your little one's unique communication style and needs.

Tailored Consultations

Facing a specific challenge in your parenting journey? Sometimes you need focused guidance for immediate concerns. These personalized sessions address your most pressing questions with compassionate, evidence-based support tailored to your unique situation.

Overnight Support

Restful nights for growing families. I provide attentive newborn care while you replenish your energy - feeding support, gentle soothing, and diaper changes all handled with loving care while you sleep.

Lactation Support & Education

Evidence-based guidance for your feeding journey, whether breastfeeding, bottle-feeding, or pumping. I help you establish confidence in nourishing your little one with patience and understanding.

Family Bonding Support

Gentle guidance for nurturing strong connections from the beginning, with special sensitivity to diverse family structures and opportunities to build the supportive network that modern parents often find missing.
